EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on January 1, 2024, between TechCorp Inc., a Delaware corporation ("Company"), and John Smith ("Employee").

SECTION 1. POSITION AND DUTIES
1.1 Employee shall serve as Senior Software Engineer and shall have such duties and responsibilities as may be assigned by the Company's management.
1.2 Employee agrees to devote their full business time and attention to the performance of duties under this Agreement.

SECTION 2. COMPENSATION
2.1 Base Salary: Company shall pay Employee a base salary of $120,000 per year, payable in bi-weekly installments.
2.2 Benefits: Employee shall be entitled to participate in all employee benefit plans maintained by the Company.

SECTION 3. TERM OF EMPLOYMENT
3.1 This Agreement shall commence on January 1, 2024, and shall continue until terminated in accordance with this Agreement.
3.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time with or without cause upon thirty (30) days written notice.

SECTION 4. CONFIDENTIALITY
4.1 Employee acknowledges that they will have access to confidential information of the Company.
4.2 Employee agrees to maintain strict confidentiality of all proprietary information during and after employment.

SECTION 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
5.1 All inventions, discoveries, and improvements made by Employee during employment shall be the exclusive property of the Company.
5.2 Employee agrees to assign all rights, title, and interest in such intellectual property to the Company.

SECTION 6. NON-COMPETE
6.1 During employment and for a period of twelve (12) months after termination, Employee shall not engage in any business that competes with the Company.
6.2 This restriction applies within a 50-mile radius of the Company's principal place of business.

SECTION 7. TERMINATION
7.1 Termination for Cause: Company may terminate this Agreement immediately for cause, including but not limited to:
    a) Breach of this Agreement
    b) Misconduct or negligence
    c) Conviction of a felony
7.2 Termination without Cause: Either party may terminate without cause with thirty (30) days written notice.
7.3 Upon termination, Employee shall return all Company property and confidential information.

SECTION 8. SEVERABILITY
8.1 If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

SECTION 9. GOVERNING LAW
9.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.
